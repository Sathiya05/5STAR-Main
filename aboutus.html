Who We Are

South India's Most Trusted Event Management Company

Established in 2008, 5 Star Event Management has grown from a small passionate team into a leading ISO 9001:2015 certified event management company. Headquartered in Hosur, we proudly serve across Tamilnadu, Kerala, Karnataka, and Andhra Pradesh, delivering exceptional event experiences for over 18 years.

From intimate private gatherings to grand destination weddings and large-scale corporate events, we combine creativity, precision, and cultural expertise to design celebrations that truly reflect your vision.

Every event we manage is driven by our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and the trust our clients place in us—creating unforgettable moments that last a lifetime.

Our Journey

A Story Built on Celebrations

2006 — The Beginning

Founded in Hosur, Tamilnadu

5 Star Event Management was established in Hosur with a vision to create extraordinary celebrations. We began by organizing weddings and private events with a strong focus on quality, creativity, and personal attention.

2012 — Expanding Horizons

Growing Across Tamilnadu

With increasing trust and demand, we expanded our services across major cities including Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, and Salem, delivering seamless event experiences throughout Tamilnadu.

2016 — Corporate Excellence

Entering Corporate Event Management

We stepped into the corporate event space, successfully managing conferences, product launches, and business events with professionalism, precision, and strong brand execution.

2020 — Quality Recognition

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Our commitment to quality and structured processes earned us ISO 9001:2015 certification, strengthening our position as a trusted event management company.

2026 — Today

5000+ Successful Events & 20+ Years of Experience

With over 20 years of experience and 5000+ successful events, 5 Star Event Management continues to deliver exceptional services across Tamilnadu, Kerala, Karnataka, and Andhra Pradesh.
